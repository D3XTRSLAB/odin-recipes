Roasted Garlic Lemon Broccoli

In the mood for some broccoli? This one is for you!

List of Ingredients

  • 2 heads of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 or 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the broccoli florets with the extra virgin olive oil, sea salt, pepper and garlic.
  3. Spread the broccoli out in an even layer on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the florets are tender enough to peirce the stems with a fork, after around 15 to 20 minutes.
  5. Once cooked, remove and place onto a serving platter and apply lemon juice liberally.
  6. Enjoy.
