Vegan Kale Soup

If you like vegetables and you like soup? Then you will definitely enjoy this warm and healthy recipe.

List of Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 diced yellow onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
  • 1 bunch of kale chopped with the stems removed
  • 8 cups of water
  • 6 cubes of vegetable boullion
  • 15 ounces of fresh or canned tomatoes
  • 6 medium sized diced potatoes
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans of cannellini beans
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons of dried parsley
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Add olive oil in a large soup pot. Bring this to a medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is warm. Add the onions and garlic then let simmer until soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in the kale and simmer untill wilted for about 2 minutes.
  4. Then add the water, vegetable boullon, tomatoes, potatoes, beans, italian seasoning, and parsley. Let simmer on medium heat for 25 minutes, or untill the potatoes are thouroughly cooked.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Enjoy.
