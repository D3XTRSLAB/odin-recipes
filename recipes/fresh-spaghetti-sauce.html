Fresh Spaghetti Sauce

Who doesnt like spaghetti? And what's better than just spaghetti? That's right spaghetti with sauce! And this is a good one. Made from fresh tomatoes. Serve this over your favorite spaghettis and don't forget the parmesan! If you desire that is.

List of Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 4 pounds of fresh peeled and chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon of dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is warm add the onion and the garlic powder. Cook this untill the onions become translucent after about 5 minutes.
  3. Then add the tomatoes, sugar, basil, parsey, and salt. Bring this to a boil.
  4. Once boiling, reduce the heat and let it simmer. Stir occasionally untill the sauce thickens, after about 1 to 2 hours.
  5. Let the sauce cool at room temperature and its ready to be served.
  6. Enjoy.
